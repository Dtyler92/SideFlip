Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SideFlip - Project Ledger is built to help you track your flips and know your profit. We take your privacy seriously and keep things simple — no ads, no selling your data, ever.

What we collect

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account
  • Project data — titles, purchase prices, expenses, notes, and photos you add to your projects
  • Payment info — handled entirely by Stripe; we never see or store your card number

How we use it

  • To provide and improve the SideFlip app
  • To send you transactional emails (receipts, account notices)
  • To generate AI-powered Facebook Marketplace listings from your project data
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers

Third-party services

We use trusted third-party services to run SideFlip:

  • Supabase — stores your account and project data securely (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — processes subscription payments (privacy policy)
  • Resend — delivers transactional emails (privacy policy)
  • Anthropic — powers the AI listing generator using your project details (privacy policy)

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data needed for them to do their job.

Photos

Photos you add to projects are stored securely in Supabase Storage and are only accessible to your account. We don't use your photos for any other purpose.

Data retention

Your data is kept as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed within 30 days. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask us to delete it, or ask any questions about how it's used by contacting us below. We'll respond within 5 business days.

Children

SideFlip is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll notify you by email or in the app.
